Data retention. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data
from our production systems within 30 days. Encrypted backups are retained
for up to 90 days, after which they are permanently erased. Aggregated,
anonymised usage statistics may be kept indefinitely for product analytics.

strictly to operate the service, under contractual data-processing terms.
